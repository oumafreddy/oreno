Risk Assessment Guidelines
Risk Levels
  • Minimal Low impact, well-understood models
  • Limited Moderate impact, some uncertainty
  • High Significant impact, high uncertainty
  • Unacceptable Prohibited for production use
Assessment Process
  1. Select the model to assess
  2. Evaluate risk factors
  3. Document mitigation measures
  4. Submit for review
  5. Obtain approval before production
Required Information
  • Risk factors and scoring
  • Mitigation measures
  • Compliance requirements
  • Evidence documents
Risk Factors Format

You can use either format:

List format:

["stakeholder review completed", "monitoring dashboard in place", "rollback plan defined"]

Dictionary format:

{
  "data_quality": {"score": 3, "description": "High quality training data"},
  "model_complexity": {"score": 2, "description": "Moderate complexity"},
  "deployment_risk": {"score": 4, "description": "High deployment risk"}
}
